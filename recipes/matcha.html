Matcha

Description

A delicious treat just like the one from Starbucks, except a lot healthier. You can use any type of milk in this reci

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ice cubes
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 1 fluid ounce vanilla-flavored syrup (such as Torani®), or to taste
  • 1 ½ teaspoons green tea powder (matcha)

Steps

  1. Heat water until it is hot but not boiling.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of matcha powder to a cup.
  3. Pour a small amount of hot water into the cup
  4. Whisk the matcha well until there are no lumps.
  5. Add more hot water as needed.
  6. Whisk again until a light foam forms on top.
  7. Serve and enjoy your matcha.
